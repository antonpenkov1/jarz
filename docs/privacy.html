Jarz — Privacy Policy

Jarz does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All information you enter — categories, amounts, transactions, settings — stays on your device and is never sent anywhere. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and makes no network requests.

If you delete the app, all of its data is deleted with it.
